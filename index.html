ABOUT TBX

We translate your research into commercially-viable technology.

TechBiz Xccelerator aka TBX is run by a group of people who are passionate about creating impact from inventions. Viktor Cheng is the chief instigator behind all this. He is motivated to find new ways to improve the rate of flow and increase the success rate of inventions making an impact. As such, he started on this journey of bringing together like-minded individuals to work together to make this happen.

Our team comprises technology development, software engineering, user experience, marketing, business development, quality assurance, project management and technical support folks who enjoy working with like-minded people, love to talk to inventors about their inventions and is passionate about creating impactful solutions for society.
